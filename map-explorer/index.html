How It Works

GenerationTiles are generated on-the-fly by a diffusion model running on a GPU. The first visit to a new area may take 10–20 seconds.
CachingOnce generated, tiles are cached — revisiting is instant.
City templatesClick a city thumbnail at the bottom to start from a real city layout.
ModelsUse the dropdown in the top-left panel to switch between trained models.
Built with a JiT flow-matching diffusion model trained on OpenStreetMap data.
Each zoom level generates 4 child tiles from the parent, forming a quadtree of infinite detail.
